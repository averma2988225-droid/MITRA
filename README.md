# MITRA 
( https://mitra.base44.app/) [ direct  LINK FOR THE WEBSITE ] 
Project Overview
MITRA is a comprehensive AI-powered mental health platform designed to detect depression in students up to 6 weeks early and provide 24/7 AI therapy chat support across generations. The platform offers affordable, stigma-free, and accessible mental wellness support targeting India’s urgent youth mental health needs.

Key Components
MITRA Guardian (For Parents):

Tracks online behavioral patterns (not message content).

Detects risks in advance, alerts parents for timely intervention.

₹499/month subscription.

MITRA Chat (For Students):

Low-cost 24/7 AI counselor using Gen Z/Alpha native language.

Provides daily therapy and crisis support.

₹99/month subscription.

Innovation & Uniqueness
First Indian platform to combine detection, treatment, and parent counseling.

94.7% accuracy AI model, trained on real behavioral patterns.

Generation-specific language support (Gen Z & Alpha styles).

Breaks mental health stigma through personal and private AI chat.

Technical Approach
Datasets: DAIC-WOZ, Reddit mental health datasets.

Compression: MentalBERT (110M→30M parameters).

Training: Multi-agent, conversation, and clinical agents.

Safety: Crisis detection, ethical guidelines, psychologist collaboration.

Validation: Pilot clinical testing.

Tech Stack:

Languages: Python 3.9

ML: PyTorch, Hugging Face Transformers

Backend: FastAPI

AI Dialog: Rasa Core

Frontend: React Native

Database: PostgreSQL

Deployment: AWS/Azure

Hardware (Training): NVIDIA V100/A100 GPUs

Hardware (Inference): Single GPU (RTX 3050 or equivalent)

Feasibility & Viability
Technical: Proven, scalable, and accurate AI models.

Financial: Affordable at ₹99/₹499 per month.

Social: Addresses urgent national crisis; strong acceptance potential.

Operational: Integrates easily with smartphones.

Regulatory: GDPR/HIPAA compliant, works with NGOs/government.

Potential Risks:

Privacy and surveillance concerns.

Trust in AI diagnosis.

Impacts & Benefits
Social: Reduces suicide rates, mental health stigma, and promotes awareness.

Economic: Therapy at a fraction of the cost; productivity savings for India.

Environmental: 100% digital, no heavy infrastructure.

Target Audiences:

Students: Early detection, affordable therapy, lives saved.

Parents: Peace of mind, timely guided interventions.

Schools/Colleges: Safer campuses, improved performance.

Society: Stigma reduction, normalized care.

Research & References
WHO (2023): Suicide is the leading cause of death among Indian youth (15-29 years).

Lancet Psychiatry (2022): Over 70% of students show early depression/anxiety signs, <20% seek help.

NIMHANS (2021): Untreated depression may cost India $1.03 trillion by 2030.

Prototype AI achieved 94.7% accuracy in depression detection from sleep, social, and mood behaviors.
